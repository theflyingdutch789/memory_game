How to Play

Welcome to the Dot Activation Game! Here's how to play:

  1. Click the "Start Game" button to begin.
  2. A series of layers with dots will appear.
  3. Your goal is to click the correct dot in each layer within the time limit.
  4. If you select the wrong dot, the game will reset to the first layer.
  5. Complete all layers before the timer runs out to win the game!

Good luck and have fun!
